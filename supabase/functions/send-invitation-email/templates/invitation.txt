## Template Variables (Same as HTML)

- `{{inviterName}}` - Name of person who sent the invitation
- `{{email}}` - Recipient's email address
- `{{roleDisplay}}` - Display name of role (`Administrator` or `Seller`)
- `{{message}}` - Optional personal message
- `{{inviteUrl}}` - Full invitation URL with token
- `{{expiryDate}}` - Formatted expiration date
- `{{isAdmin}}` - Boolean flag to show admin-specific content
